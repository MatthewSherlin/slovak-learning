## Skipped

- 46.jpg (Ahoj — hi/bye): Skipped. A generic greeting wave is too ambiguous and any image would be misleading or generic; monogram fallback preferred.
- 47.jpg (Čau — bye casual): Skipped. Same reason as Ahoj; casual goodbye has no distinct visual beyond a hand wave, which is already Ahoj's territory.
- 48.jpg (Pohoda — chill/no worries): Skipped. "Chill/relaxed" is a mood that can be conveyed by many unrelated paintings; no single image is obviously correct without looking random.
- 49.jpg (Dobre — good/ok): Skipped. A thumbs-up or "OK" expression portrait would look generic and potentially confused with other meanings.
- 50.jpg (Fajn — fine): Skipped. "Fine" is too vague; any smiling portrait would be misleading or indistinguishable from other positive expressions.
- 51.jpg (Jasné — sure/clear): Skipped. "Sure/clear" has no strong distinct visual analog; a lightbulb or clear sky image would not read as an expression of agreement.
- 52.jpg (Super — great): Skipped. "Great/super" is an exclamation too generic to illustrate distinctively; any celebratory image risks looking like Paráda.
- 53.jpg (Paráda — awesome): Skipped. "Awesome" is similar to Super; a celebration scene is too generic for this set and could confuse the two cards.
- 54.jpg (Mať maslo na hlave — guilty conscience): Skipped. No suitable public domain image found that clearly illustrates a guilty conscience without being misleading; "butter on head" literal interpretation yields nothing usable, and general "guilt" paintings were not found on Wikimedia with strong clarity.
